## 🎥 Recording Tips

### **Setup:**
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (HD)
- **Frame rate**: 30fps
- **Tool**: QuickTime Screen Recording or Loom
- **Audio**: Built-in mic is fine, record in quiet room

### **Preparation:**
1. **Clean desktop** - minimal distractions
2. **Sample images** - 5-10 HEIC photos ready to convert
3. **Practice run** - rehearse 2-3 times before recording

### **Visual Style:**
- **Cursor highlights** - make cursor movements obvious
- **Clean interface** - hide desktop clutter
- **Fast paced** - keep energy high, no dead time
- **Text overlays** - use white text with drop shadow

### **Voiceover Style:**
- **Confident tone** - you're solving a real problem
- **Pace**: Slightly faster than normal speech
- **Emphasize**: "instantly", "private", "$9", "3x faster"
